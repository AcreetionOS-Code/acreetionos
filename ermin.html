Project Ermin

Ermin is our vision for a permanent, independent home for the AcreetionOS community. As Discord's policies become more restrictive, we are building a platform that prioritizes user freedom and technical integrity.

Why Ermin?

  • Independence: No more reliance on Big Tech platforms and their shifting policies.
  • Customization: Tailored specifically for the needs of our community and developers.
  • Privacy: Built with the same commitment to privacy that drives AcreetionOS.

Progress Update

Project Ermin is currently in the early stages of development. We are taking a slow, deliberate approach to ensure the final product is stable and secure. We estimate a multi-year migration process as we transition away from Discord.
