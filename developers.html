Developer Portal

AcreetionOS is an open-source project. We welcome contributions from developers of all backgrounds. Our infrastructure is built on GitLab and Forgejo.

How to Contribute

  • Source Code: Visit our GitLab to browse our repositories.
  • Infrastructure: We manage over 40 Docker services. If you're an expert in DevOps or self-hosting, we could use your help.
  • Testing: Help us test our ISOs and report bugs on our Discord server.
